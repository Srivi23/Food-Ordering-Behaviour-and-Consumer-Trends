# Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends: A Structured Analysis of Choices and Habits

## Overview

The **Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends** project is a data analytics solution developed using **Tableau** to analyze customer ordering patterns, delivery performance, and consumer preferences in the online food delivery industry. The project transforms raw food ordering data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and visualizations, enabling businesses to make informed decisions.

The analysis focuses on understanding customer behaviour, identifying popular cuisines and meal types, evaluating delivery efficiency, and comparing order trends across different cities. The insights generated help improve operational performance, customer satisfaction, and business growth.

## Problem Statement

The food delivery industry generates a large amount of customer and operational data every day. However, businesses often struggle to identify meaningful patterns that influence customer preferences, order demand, delivery performance, and revenue. Without proper analysis, decision-making becomes difficult and opportunities for business improvement may be missed.

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer food ordering behaviour.
* Identify popular cuisines and meal preferences.
* Compare order trends across different cities.
* Evaluate delivery performance and efficiency.
* Discover peak ordering periods and customer demand.
* Support business decision-making through interactive Tableau dashboards.

## Dataset Information

The project uses a structured food ordering dataset containing the following attributes:

* Order ID
* Order Value
* Delivery Fee
* Cuisine
* Meal Type
* City
* Time Taken for Delivery

## Key Features
